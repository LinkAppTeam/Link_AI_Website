Profile information and user interactions may be processed using AI systems to:

We use your location (provided directly or selected by you) to match you with nearby users. We do not track precise real-time location unless explicitly stated.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

You may: Access or update your information, delete your account, or contact us for data-related requests.
