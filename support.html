How does AutoMatch work? ▼
Every week, Link AI analyses profiles in your area — comparing values, personality, and lifestyle — to select one highly compatible match for you. New matches drop weekly.
Why are photos blurred in AutoMatch? ▼
To encourage genuine connections, AutoMatch photos stay blurred until both users have exchanged 3 messages. After that, profiles are fully revealed.
What are LinkBucks / AI credits? ▼
You receive daily AI credits to use for message generation, conversation openers, and chat analysis. You can earn them daily or purchase more in-app.
How do I delete my account? ▼
You can delete your account from the Profile → Settings screen in the app. All your data will be permanently removed. If you need assistance, email us.
